Heritage Meets Innovation

Our legacy is rooted in family recipes, but our vision is set on the future of flavour, sustainability, and community.

Founding Our Bakery

1993

Two brothers began handcrafting mooncakes in Ho Chi Minh City, igniting a passion for delighting families during Mid-Autumn.

Expanding the Kinh Do Family

2000s

We introduced breads, biscuits, and confections that quickly became household staples across Vietnam and beyond.

Global Recognition

2015

Kinh Do mooncakes were celebrated internationally, highlighting Vietnamese craftsmanship on the world stage.

Tomorrow's Taste

Today

We innovate with plant-forward recipes, mindful packaging, and digital experiences that bring communities closer.

What Guides Our Craft

  • Respect for heritage techniques passed down through generations.
  • Curating premium ingredients from trusted Vietnamese growers.
  • Collaborating with chefs and designers to shape new festive rituals.
  • Supporting local communities through seasonal celebrations and giving programs.

Savour the Future Responsibly

We champion mindful sourcing and thoughtful design, ensuring every bite honours both tradition and tomorrow.

Local Ingredients

Eco Packaging

Our boxes are crafted from recycled fibres, soy-based inks, and modular inserts that reduce single-use materials.

Community Programs

Festive giving drives support education and nutrition initiatives, spreading joy beyond the holiday table.

Zero Waste Bakeries
